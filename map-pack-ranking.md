## The 8 Ranking Factors Google Uses for Cannabis Map Pack in 2026

Google looks at dozens of signals, but these eight carry the most weight for dispensaries:

### 1. Google Business Profile Strength
Complete profile, correct primary category, weekly posts, full services list, Q&A section populated, and consistent activity. Google rewards businesses that treat their GBP like a living marketing channel.

### 2. Review Velocity & Quality
Recent reviews matter far more than total count. A dispensary with 45 reviews and 8 new ones this month will usually beat one with 180 reviews but no new activity in 60 days.

### 3. Citation Consistency
NAP (Name, Address, Phone) must match perfectly across 50+ directories. Even small mismatches kill trust and suppress rankings.

### 4. Geotagged Photos & Visual Content
Photos with proper location data are powerful local signals. Google loves businesses that upload fresh, geotagged images regularly.

### 5. Website Authority & Relevance
Your actual website must support the GBP. Strong on-page SEO, LocalBusiness schema, fast load times, and relevant content all feed into local ranking.

### 6. Behavioral Signals
Click-through rate, time spent on site, direction requests, and phone calls from the Map Pack all tell Google your listing is valuable.

### 7. Proximity & Location Data
While not the only factor, physical distance still plays a role. Accurate location data and service-area settings matter.

### 8. Entity Strength
How well Google understands your business as a real, authoritative cannabis dispensary. This comes from consistent mentions, content clusters, and brand signals across the web.
